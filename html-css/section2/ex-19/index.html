Pathaan

Yash Raj Films Presents

"Pathan" is a high-octane action thriller starring Shah Rukh Khan, Deepika Padukone, and John Abraham.

About

"Pathan" is one of the highly anticipated Bollywood films known for its star-studded cast and thrilling storyline. Directed by Siddharth Anand, the movie features Shah Rukh Khan in the lead role, marking his return to the big screen after a hiatus. He is joined by leading actresses Deepika Padukone and John Abraham, who is reportedly playing a negative character. The film is expected to be an action-packed extravaganza with cutting-edge stunts and intense sequences. "Pathan" is a part of the Yash Raj Films (YRF) production house and is likely to be a significant release in the Indian film industry, generating considerable excitement among fans and film enthusiasts.
